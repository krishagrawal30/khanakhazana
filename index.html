About Us

Welcome to KhanaKhazana, your destination for the wonderful world of Indian vegetarian food. Since 2011, we've been passionate about sharing the fantastic flavors of Indian cuisine with food lovers everywhere.

Our menu is full of tasty and diverse vegetarian options. From hearty and flavorful dishes to delightful bites, every plate carries the essence of India's rich culinary landscape.

Our team is made up of passionate chefs who love creating delicious food. Beyond serving great meals, we're also committed to choices that are good for our environment and community.

Visit us and explore the delicious world of Indian vegetarian cuisine. We promise a dining experience filled with great food and good times.
